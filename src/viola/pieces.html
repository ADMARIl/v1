Popular Compositions for Viola

If you play viola, enjoy listening to the viola (haha), or just want to know more, here is a short list of some popular pieces composed for the viola

Walton Viola Concerto Written by William Walton in 1929, this concerto was composed for Lionel Tertis. Tertis, however, rejected the work and it was premiered instead by Paul Hindemith in London.
walton
Telemann Concerto in G major Among the most famous of Georg Philipp Telemann's concertos, this concerto is the first known for viola, having been written around 1716-1721.
telemann
Hoffmeister Concerto in D major This is one of the numerous concertos written by Franz Anton Hoffmeister, and is a very popular piece to be played on the viola. It was written
hoffmeister
Stamitz Concerto in D major This concerto was written by Carl Stamitz and is frequently performed in exams and auditions. Because of this, many violists don't like it. It was written in the Classical period.
stamitz
Bartók Viola Concerto One of the last pieces written by Béla Bartók, this concerto was composed in New York in 1945. The piece was comissioned by William Primrose, who was looking for a challenging piece to perform.
bartok
